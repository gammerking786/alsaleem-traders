About Al Saleem Traders

Established in 1999, Al Saleem Traders has grown to become one of the most trusted and respected chemical trading companies in Pakistan. Based in the heart of Karachi, we specialize in supplying a wide range of industrial chemicals to manufacturers, workshops, and production plants across the nation. Our journey began with a single goal — to deliver the highest-quality products with transparency, reliability, and long-term value.

Over the years, we have built a robust network of suppliers and clients through consistency, innovation, and trust. We provide premium-grade solvents, foaming agents, mould release agents, resins, coloring pastes, and several specialty formulations used across multiple industrial sectors. Whether it’s plastic manufacturing, polyurethane production, paint formulation, or rubber processing, our materials support quality at every stage.

We believe that chemical trading is not merely about supply — it’s about partnership. Our technical experts continuously explore new solutions to help clients optimize performance, reduce waste, and maintain international standards of safety. Al Saleem Traders represents the fusion of experience and modern innovation, serving industries with materials that make processes smoother and products stronger.

Our facilities are strategically located near Shoe Market, Bagh-e-Halar Hospital, Karachi, ensuring fast delivery and efficient logistics management. Every product we deliver passes through careful inspection and meets industry benchmarks for consistency. As a family-run business, we value honesty, responsibility, and commitment — the same principles that have guided us for over two decades.

Today, Al Saleem Traders stands proud as a symbol of quality and trust in Pakistan’s chemical sector. With a diverse portfolio ranging from base solvents like Butyl Acetate, Ethyl Acetate, Methylene Chloride to specialty additives such as Azodicarbonamide, Di-Octyl Phthalate, and advanced Mould Release Agents, we continue to lead the market in versatility and customer satisfaction.

Looking ahead, our vision is to expand into eco-friendly and next-generation chemical technologies while maintaining the same excellence that has defined us since 1999. We thank our loyal customers and partners for their continued trust — together, we will build a future of innovation, sustainability, and chemical mastery.
